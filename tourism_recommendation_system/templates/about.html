ℹ️ About This Project

TourMate AI

A portfolio-grade Data Science project combining Machine Learning, NLP, and Generative AI to solve real-world tourism recommendation challenges.

🎯 Problem Statement

China's tourism industry generates millions of visits annually, but travelers often struggle to discover destinations that truly match their personal preferences, budget constraints, and travel style. Generic recommendation systems based solely on popularity fail to capture the nuanced preferences of individual travelers.

TourMate AI addresses this by building a hybrid recommendation engine that combines content-based filtering, popularity signals, budget compatibility, seasonal patterns, and Generative AI explanations — delivering personalized, explainable recommendations at scale.
